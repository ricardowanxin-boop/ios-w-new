- what would need to be true for it to work
- what makes it stronger or weaker than adjacent alternatives

- pain intensity: 1-10
- frequency: 1-10
- willingness to pay: 1-10
- implementation risk: 1-10
- competition intensity: 1-10
- evidence confidence: 1-10

Output:
- eliminate weak ideas aggressively
- keep only the top 3
- produce a ranked shortlist

- /research/evidence/YYYY-MM-DD-validated-opportunities.md

Rules:
- Be skeptical
